Food: A stored item that represents any food, for example, 'brown bread', 'oats' etc. 

Meal: An item that is made up of food items and can be saved for future use.

MealItem: Food item owned by a meal that has been multiplied by user stated grams to give correct macros.

LogEntry: A record of consumption. Can either be a single food item or a meal, logs food items, macros, time and date.

DayLog: A collection of entries for a given date with totals
